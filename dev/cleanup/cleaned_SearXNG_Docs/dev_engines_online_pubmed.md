# PubMed
[PubMed](https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/) comprises more than 39 million citations for biomedical literature from MEDLINE, life science journals, and online books. Citations may include links to full text content from PubMed Central and publisher web sites.
## Configuration
```
- name: pubmed
  engine: pubmed
  shortcut: pub

```
